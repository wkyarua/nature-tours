About Naturewise Tours

Naturewise Tours is a premier East African safari operator specializing in sustainable and immersive travel experiences in Tanzania and Kenya. Unlike standard tour operators, Naturewise focuses on "Explore the Earth, Respect its Worth," ensuring that every trip contributes to conservation efforts and local community welfare.

The company offers a diverse range of products including luxury photographic safaris in the Serengeti, budget-friendly camping trips in Tarangire, and high-altitude trekking expeditions on Mount Kilimanjaro. With a fleet of custom 4x4 Land Cruisers and professional, multilingual guides, Naturewise ensures safety, comfort, and deep educational value for every guest.

Destination Highlights

🇹🇿 Tanzania

  • Serengeti National Park: Famous for the Great Migration of over 1.5 million wildebeest.
  • Ngorongoro Crater: The world's largest inactive volcanic caldera, home to the Big 5.
  • Tarangire National Park: Known for its massive elephant herds and iconic Baobab trees.
  • Zanzibar: A historic archipelago offering pristine beaches and cultural Stone Town tours.

🇰🇪 Kenya

  • Maasai Mara: The northern extension of the Serengeti, offering incredible predator action.
  • Amboseli National Park: Famous for huge tuskers (elephants) with Mt. Kilimanjaro backdrops.
  • Lake Nakuru: A birdwatcher's paradise, famous for flamingos and rhinos.
  • Mount Kenya: A challenging technical climb and a UNESCO World Heritage site.
